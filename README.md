# American Mahjong — 2026 Card

A complete, single-file American Mahjong (Mah Jongg) game for the browser. You play against
three computer opponents using the 2026 National Mah Jongg League card.

**Play it:** just open `index.html` in any modern browser — no install, no server, no dependencies.

## Features

- Full 2026 NMJL card: all 9 sections and 55 hands with suit variants, concealed flags, and values
- Complete rules: full Charleston (both rounds, blind pass, courtesy pass), claim windows with
  Hold/Release, joker rules and joker exchanges, concealed hands, wall games
- NMJL money scoring: discarder pays double, self-draw doubles, jokerless bonus, running totals
- Three bot difficulty levels (Easy / Standard / Expert) with human-like hand targeting
- Optional house rule: play with blanks (6 blanks + 2 extra jokers, swap a blank for any dead discard)
- Interactive score card with live per-hand progress, plus a pinnable "target hands" workspace
- Smart sort (arranges your rack to match your pinned target hand), drag-to-rearrange rack
- Three tile sets (Classic, America 250, Crimson Tide) and three tables (Green Felt,
  4th of July, Houndstooth) under Menu → Customization
- Progress and settings persist between sessions in your browser

## How to play

1. Open `index.html`
2. Pick bot difficulty and options on the start menu, then Start Game
3. During the Charleston, click 3 tiles and pass; decide on the second Charleston and courtesy pass
4. On your turn: click a tile twice (or use Discard) to throw; claim discards during the
   8-second window with Pung / Kong / Quint / Mahjong — or Hold to think it over
5. Pin hands on the score card to track them in the workspace above it
